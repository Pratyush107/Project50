Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camels can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts About Teddies:

  1. The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846.
  2. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  3. The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas:

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  2. Most have red-orange eyes.
  3. Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.

Interesting Facts About Pandas:

  1. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  2. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
  3. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
